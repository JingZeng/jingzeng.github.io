Print Design




MAGAZINE AD


This is one of my freelance work - a magazine ad for WHM Tractors published on Power Farming Vol.127 in 2017.

It is an 226mm by 330mm foldout page with 5mm bleed. The main colours used in the AD are red and blue, which is in accordance with the company logo. Overall, I aim to design an AD which looks professional and presents a positive image of the company.


Layout Design

Brief

I was challenged to design a 12-page digital booklet using only colour and form. I chose a few simple shapes - circles, curves and lines, together with lots of negative spaces, and a light colour palette, to present this harmonious concept.







Menu Design



design process

This price list was designed for a local pet grooming shop. It is a re-design of their previous menu.

I re-designed the visual hierarchy, so the new version delivers information more efficiently to the customers. The main colour I chose is yellow/orange contrasting a dark background, which conveys a professional yet warm feeling. The fonts I used are Creative Block and Sweetness, which looks lively and adorable, just like the shop name 'peppy puppy'.

I designed this photobook for a food artist. All the photos are selected from her Instagram and placed into different shapes and layouts.
