SketchPad

DESIGN BRIEF

I designed this sketchpad, which allows users to customise their own patterns on a T-shirt. Compared with those complicated drawing software like photoshop or Illustrator, this sketchpad is simple to use, especially for those who do not have much drawing skills. The target customers are young people who want to wear cool and unique clothes.

FEATURES

  • Switch between black and white T-shirt.
  • Five different colourful brush tools with unique patterns, easy to create beautiful styles with no drawing skills required.
  • Simultaneously preview on T-shirt and export your ready-to-print design.

FOCUS: Design Programming

TOOLS: Processing
