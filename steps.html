STEPs

DESIGN BRIEF

We were challenged to design an online Web-based goal-setting tool that allows primary school aged children to set goals with the support of their teacher and parents. The motivation for providing such a tool should be based on the idea of personalised learning.


OUR SOLUTION

We built this web-based Personalised Learning Plan (PLP) named STEPs, which incorporates gamification concept into goal achievement process. Our website enables students to timely and easily set personalised goals, view progress, receive suggestions from their teachers and parents and also compete with other students.

Every achievement is visualized as a footprint on the virtual map, which makes the learning progress less tedious, more playful and motivational.

Features

STEPs encourages students to logically and efficiently set and achieve personal interested, as well as enjoying the gaming system, and communicates with friends, parents and teacher.

In the web prototype, we focused on the main function - the process of setting and managing goals. By following our Setting Goal processes, students will learn to think and analyses things from an individual events. Then the long-time achievements and statistic results of goal categories (current, completed, on-hold, dropped goals) help to build up personal time and interest schedule via critical thinking and analysing skills.


Reflection and Next Step

Limitations

Due to a limited time, there are a number of aspects in our design that we haven't fully developed. We tried to present as many details as we could, however there were too many small features that required more time and effort to work on. For example, this website is not responsive to mobile and tablet screens at this moment. Also due to lacking database supported, it is more like a demonstration website, instead of allowing users' interactions.


Next Step
  • Develop a mobile/tablet version for this website, using responsive design framework such as Bootstrap.
  • Build up a database and allow users to actually sign up and interact on this website.
  • Refines lots of details and functions such as transitions, photo slides, layouts, etc.
