STEPs

Web Design

We built this web-based Personalised Learning Plan (PLP) named STEPs, which incorporates gamification concept into the goal achievement process. Our website enables the students to timely and easily set personalised goals, view the progress, receive suggestions from their teachers and parents and also compete with other students.

Every achievement is visualized as a footprint on the virtual map, which makes the learning progress less tedious, more playful and motivational.
