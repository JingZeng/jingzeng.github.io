Catch My Heart

DESIGN BRIEF

We designed this hand-held game device to bring light-hearted entertainment to players. The goal of this game is to collect the heart shape by pressing the button, so that the guy on the left can move towards the girl on the far right. Random shapes appear and change faster every time you press the button. You win when the two characters meet, or lose when you press on wrong shapes.


DESIGN TEAM

I collaborated with two design students on this project. My role was the game designer and developer.
