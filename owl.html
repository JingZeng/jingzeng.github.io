TUMBLER OWL MONEY BOX




DESIGN BRIEF

I was challenged to design a children's toy by 3D modelling and fabrication.


My SOLUTION

I designed a Tumbler Owl Saving Box for 6+ age. It is playful as well as cultivating saving habits. The eyes of the owl were 3D printed and the body was built through stacking and waffling the laser-cut plywood pieces.


FOCUS: 3D modelling and fabrication

TOOLS: Rhinoceros 3D | Laser Cutting, stacking and waffling

DATE: Aug-Nov 2016
